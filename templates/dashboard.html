🛡️ Cancer Prevention: Stay One Step Ahead

🥦 Healthy Diet

Eat fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid processed foods and sugar.

🏃 Stay Active

Exercise for 30 minutes daily to maintain a healthy weight and reduce cancer risks.

🚭 Avoid Tobacco

Smoking and tobacco increase the risk of lung, throat, and other cancers.

☀️ Sun Protection

Wear sunscreen (SPF 30+), hats, and sunglasses to prevent skin cancer.

💉 Get Vaccinated

HPV and Hepatitis B vaccines help prevent cervical and liver cancer.

🏥 Regular Check-ups

Early detection of cancer saves lives. Get routine screenings as recommended.

🛌 Sleep & Stress Management

7-8 hours of sleep and stress management reduce cancer risks.

⚠️ Avoid Harmful Chemicals

Reduce exposure to pollutants, pesticides, and harmful chemicals.
